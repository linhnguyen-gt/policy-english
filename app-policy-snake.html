Privacy Policy for Snake Hunting Prey

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how the personal information of users ("User," "you," or "your") is collected, used, and disclosed by Snake Hunting Prey ("we," "us," or "our") in connection with the "Snake Hunting Prey" mobile application ("App"). By downloading, accessing, or playing our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our App, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy. The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Third-Party Service Providers

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the App:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This service collects information such as:

  • App usage patterns and frequency
  • Game mode preferences and play sessions
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

This data is collected anonymously and is used to improve the App's features and user experience. You can opt out of Analytics data collection through your device settings.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information when the App encounters errors. This helps us identify and fix bugs to improve App stability. Crash reports may include:

  • Device state at the time of crash
  • Operating system version and device model
  • App state and recent user actions before the crash
  • Stack traces and error logs

Crash data is collected automatically and does not include personally identifiable information. This data is used solely for debugging and improving App performance.

Advertising

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads based on your interests. This may include:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage information

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our App, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our App, the time and date of your use of the App, and other statistics.

Local Data Storage

The App stores certain data locally on your device to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Game Scores: Your high scores and game history are stored locally on your device.
  • Game Settings: Your preferences such as sound, haptic feedback, and difficulty settings.
  • Cosmetic Items: Purchased or unlocked skins and themes.
  • In-Game Currency: Coins earned through gameplay or watching ads.

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services unless explicitly stated.

Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

Analytics and crash data are retained according to the retention policies of our third-party service providers (Google/Firebase). Local data on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear App data through your device settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Opt out of analytics data collection

To exercise these rights, you can adjust your device settings or contact us directly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
